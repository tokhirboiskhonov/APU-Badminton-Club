Get ready to rally, smash, and celebrate the sheer joy of badminton as we proudly present the inaugural APU Sudirman Cup! Inspired by the prestigious BWF Sudirman Cup, this exciting event is exclusively tailored for our talented APU Badminton Club members.

Event Overview:

The APU Sudirman Cup aims to bring the exhilarating spirit of international badminton competitions right to our school grounds. As we gear up for this grand event, it's not just a tournament â€“ it's a celebration of skill, teamwork, and the unbridled passion for the shuttlecock sport that unites us all.

Competition Format:

Exclusively designed for APU Badminton Club members, the Sudirman Cup will feature a thrilling team format. Players will form teams, each representing different regions, fostering a sense of camaraderie and unity. This team-centric approach not only echoes the global Sudirman Cup format but also encourages collaboration among our club members.

Who Can Participate:

This competition is an exclusive opportunity for APU Badminton Club members to showcase their prowess on the court. Whether you're a seasoned player or a rising star, the APU Sudirman Cup invites you to be part of an unforgettable badminton experience.
